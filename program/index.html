    Xilei’s Team (Person from PHEER?)
  • 3:30 PM - 3:35 PM The moderator introduces the speakers and outlines the session theme
  • 3:35 PM - 3:55 PM Task Force Formation Presentation (Presenter from PHEER or Xilei’s Team) - an introduction to task forces, the principles for forming effective task forces, the various roles within a task force, and an overview of relevant case studies.
  • 3:55 PM - 4:10 PM Audience Q&A and Open Discussion
  • 4:10 PM - 4:30 PM Group Activity: Participants will break into small groups, each assigned a specific disaster scenario (e.g., wildfire, hurricane, earthquake). Within their groups, members will work together to identify the roles needed in their task force, define the responsibilities associated with each role, and discuss how they will collaborate on tasks. Finally, each group will outline an action plan for their scenario.
  • 4:30 PM - 4:50 PM Group Reports: Each group will share the key takeaways from their discussions, followed by a summary of these points provided by the moderator.
  • 4:50 PM - 5:00 PM Moderator summarizes key takeaways and closes the session
